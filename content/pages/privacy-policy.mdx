## Introduction

Excel Sequence Generator is a simple web tool for creating Excel formulas, row labels, and CSV-style previews from values you enter in the page.

This first version is designed as a no-login, no-payment, no-file-upload utility. It does not need access to your Excel workbook.

## Information We Process

When you use the generator, you may enter values such as a prefix, starting number, row count, step size, separator, or padding preference. These inputs are used to produce formulas and previews for you.

We do not ask you to upload spreadsheet files. Please do not enter confidential, regulated, or sensitive personal information into the tool.

## Analytics and Cookies

We may use basic analytics or cookies to understand aggregate usage, improve page quality, and keep the site reliable. Analytics data, if enabled, is intended to be aggregated and not used to identify individual spreadsheet content.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited technical or analytics data with service providers that help us host, secure, monitor, or improve the website.

## Security

We use reasonable technical measures to operate the website safely. No internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, so you should treat generated formulas and pasted values as your own responsibility before using them in production workflows.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the site adds new features. When we do, we will update this page.
